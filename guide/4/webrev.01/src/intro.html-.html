 20 <p> When the JDK was released under the GPLv2 license in May 2007, the need for
 21 a detailed document for contributors was anticipated.  Translating rather
 22 sophisticated institutional knowledge into a format suitable for non-Oracle
 23 employees is always a challenge.  This document attempts to address the needs
 24 of developers who wish to participate in the future of the JDK. </p>
 25 
 26 <p> Comments may be sent to <a
 27 href="mailto:guide-discuss(at)openjdk.java.net">guide-discuss (at)
 28 openjdk.java.net</a>.  </p>
