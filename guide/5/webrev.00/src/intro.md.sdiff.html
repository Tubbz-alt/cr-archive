11 When the JDK was released under the GPLv2 license in May 2007, the need for a
12 detailed document for contributors was anticipated. Translating rather
13 sophisticated institutional knowledge into a format suitable for non-Oracle
14 employees is always a challenge. This document attempts to address the needs of
15 developers who wish to participate in the future of the JDK.
